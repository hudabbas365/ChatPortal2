# AI Insights v1 — Bringing Your Data to Life with SQL & Power BI

Today we’re excited to share AI Insights v1.

This release is all about one goal: helping teams get answers from their data faster, without waiting on SQL tickets, dashboard backlogs, or manual report requests. With v1, we’re focusing on two high-impact integrations that many teams already rely on every day:

- **SQL Online Service**
- **Power BI Semantic Models**

From day one, we wanted AI Insights to feel practical. Not “AI for AI’s sake,” but a real productivity layer for operations, finance, sales, customer success, and leadership teams.

## Why v1 starts with SQL and Power BI

Most organizations already have important business data in SQL databases and curated metrics in Power BI semantic models. So instead of asking teams to migrate or re-model everything, we designed AI Insights to connect directly to what they already trust.

With SQL Online Service, teams can explore table-based data in plain English.  
With Power BI Semantic Models, they can ask questions against established business definitions and measures.

That means less setup overhead and faster time-to-value.

## What you can do right now

Once connected, users can:

- Ask natural-language questions about performance, trends, and outliers
- Get instant AI-generated answers with visual charts
- Add results directly to dashboards
- Share report views with stakeholders

Here are a few example questions teams are already asking:

- “What were our top 10 customers by revenue last quarter?”
- “Compare this month’s conversion rate to the previous 3 months.”
- “Which product categories are growing fastest year-over-year?”
- “Show me churn risk by region.”
- “What changed most in support ticket volume this week?”

The point is simple: users ask business questions, and AI Insights returns understandable answers quickly.

## Building for business users, not just technical teams

We come from data engineering and analytics backgrounds, so we know the usual pattern:

1. Business asks a question  
2. Data team writes SQL / updates BI  
3. Stakeholders wait  
4. Follow-up questions restart the cycle

AI Insights v1 helps break that cycle.

Business users can self-serve routine questions. Data teams can spend less time on repetitive ad-hoc requests and more time on strategic data modeling and quality improvements. Everyone gets faster alignment because insights are easier to access and share.

## Dashboard and report experience

In v1, we also focused on making report building smoother and more flexible:

- Easy chart and table composition
- Multi-page dashboard/report layouts
- Theme controls for consistent visual style
- Shareable report views
- Link/navigation widgets for guided storytelling

This makes it easier for teams to move from one-off questions to reusable reporting experiences.

## Security and trust

When it comes to analytics, trust matters as much as speed.

AI Insights is built on a modern stack and designed for enterprise use with governance in mind. We aim to keep data access controlled, responses explainable, and usage patterns transparent so organizations can confidently scale adoption.
